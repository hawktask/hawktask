# 12. Governance & Decision-Making

**Project:** Hawktask  
**Owner:** Executive Sponsor / PMO  
**Status:** Planning

---

## Steering Committee

**Roles:** Executive Sponsor (strategy, budget), Product Owner (vision, prioritization), Technical Lead (architecture, tech), Project Lead (execution, coordination), Finance/Business (budget, ROI).  
**Cadence:** Monthly (e.g. 1h).  
**Agenda:** Strategy, budget and timeline status, risks, major decisions, next period priorities.

---

## Decision Authority Matrix

| Decision Type | Owner | Approval | Timeline |
|---------------|-------|----------|----------|
| Technical architecture | Tech Lead | Steering (major changes) | 1–2 days |
| Feature prioritization | Product Owner | Project Lead | 3–5 days |
| Scope changes | Project Lead | Steering (Tier 3) | Per Section 3 |
| Budget changes | Finance | Executive Sponsor | 1–2 weeks |
| Timeline changes | Project Lead | Steering | 1 week |
| Security decisions | Security / Tech Lead | Tech Lead + Steering if material | 2–3 days |

**Levels:** Team (day-to-day tech); Tech Lead (architecture, security); Project Lead (resources, timeline, process); Steering (strategy, budget, major scope).

---

## Escalation

**Immediate:** Security incidents, critical outages, budget overrun &gt;10%, timeline slip &gt;2 weeks, key person loss.  
**Standard:** Decision blocked at current level; impact exceeds authority; stakeholder conflict; resource constraints.

**Process:** Document issue (what, tried, impact, recommendation) → escalate to owner → decision and rationale recorded → communicated.

---

## Phase Gate Reviews

- **Gate 1 (after Foundation):** Planning and architecture approved; budget and team allocated.  
- **Gate 2 (after MVP):** MVP complete; 50+ users; security baseline; go-ahead for Automation.  
- **Gate 3 (after Automation):** 500+ users; $50K+ trajectory; product-market fit signals; go-ahead for Scale.  
- **Gate 4 (after Scale):** 5,000+ users; $150K+ ARR path; NPS ≥ 40; decision on Series A or profitability.

Criteria from Section 2 and 6; go/no-go by Steering.

---

## Conflict Resolution

- Technical/product disagreement: Product and Tech Lead align; escalate to Steering if unresolved.  
- Scope or priority: Product Owner with Project Lead; Tier 2/3 per Section 3.  
- Resource or budget: Finance and Executive Sponsor with Project Lead.
