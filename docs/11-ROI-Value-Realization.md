# 11. ROI & Value Realization

**Project:** Hawktask  
**Owner:** Business Lead  
**Status:** Planning

---

## ROI Formula

**ROI = (Net Value − Investment Cost) / Investment Cost × 100%**  
Net Value = benefits − ongoing costs. Investment = development + infra + tools + personnel.

---

## Investment Costs (from external docs)

**Infrastructure (monthly):**  
- MVP: ~$51 (Vercel $20, Supabase $25, Hetzner $6).  
- 1K users: ~$150/mo.  
- 10K users: ~$800/mo (Architecture).

**Personnel and tools:** Per Section 7; contingency 15–20%.

---

## Business Value and Revenue (from PRD, Business-model)

**Freemium tiers:**  
- Free: 3 clients, limited proposals/channels.  
- Pro: $19/mo, unlimited clients, full automation.  
- Business: $49/mo, team seats, advanced features.

**Targets (PRD, Business-model):**  
- Month 6: 5,000 registered users; 15% conversion → 750 Pro.  
- Month 12: 750 Pro × $19/mo → ~$14,250 MRR → ~$171K ARR.  
- CAC &lt; $50; LTV &gt; $228 (12-month avg); LTV:CAC &gt; 3:1.

**User value:** 60% reduction in proposal time, 80% in invoice chase, 40% increase in testimonial collection (PRD).

---

## 30-60-90 Day ROI Tracking

- **30 days:** MVP live; 50+ users; validate value (first proposal sent, limits felt).  
- **60 days:** 500+ users; $50K+ revenue/partnership trajectory; conversion and retention trends.  
- **90 days:** 5,000+ users; $150K+ ARR path; unit economics and payback.

---

## Unit Economics

- **CAC:** Target &lt;$50 (content, community, paid).  
- **LTV:** &gt;$228 (12-month retention assumption).  
- **LTV:CAC:** &gt;3:1.  
- **Payback:** &lt;12 months target.

---

## Sensitivity and Contingency

- **Conversion below 15%:** Improve onboarding and upgrade prompts; review free tier value.  
- **CAC above $50:** Optimize channels; focus on organic and community.  
- **Churn &gt;5%:** Customer success and health scores (Customer Support); product and support improvements.
